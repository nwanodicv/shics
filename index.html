We are SHICS Modern School Offering girls and boys aged 3-14 a strategically designed education with faith and exellence.

I am delighted to welcome you to our website and hope it offers you a genuine sense of what life is like at Sacred Harvesters International Christian School. With a history spanning more than one years, our school is rich in tradition, culture, and heritage—values that are deeply rooted in our community. Alongside this remarkable history, however, is a vibrant and forward-looking spirit. We take great pride in combining traditional values with a modern approach, supported by excellent facilities, a dynamic learning environment, and a school culture where children are encouraged to simply be children. Sacred Harvesters International Christian School is a truly special place, and it is a privilege to serve as Headmaster of such a distinguished independent school. While our website provides a helpful introduction to who we are, nothing compares to experiencing the school in person. I warmly invite you to visit us for a tour or taster day at any time, where you can discover for yourself what makes SHICS such an exceptional place to learn. My team and I look forward to welcoming you.

Staff Dashboard

The Sacred Harvesters International Christian School Staff Dashboard is designed to support our dedicated teachers and staff by providing a simple, secure, and efficient way to manage daily activities. This platform helps streamline attendance, lesson note submissions, and access to important staff information.

Details of Staff Dashboard

The Staff Dashboard serves as your central workspace within the Sacred Harvesters International Christian School Dashboard. From here, you can view your personal information, monitor your attendance history, and manage your daily academic responsibilities. The dashboard is designed to be simple and user-friendly, ensuring that you can focus more on teaching and less on administrative processes. All data displayed here is securely managed and updated by the school administration.

What you can Do

As a staff member, you can easily view your attendance records, upload lesson notes, and stay informed about your work history within the school. The system ensures that your attendance is accurately recorded by the administration, promoting transparency and accountability.

What you can Do

This platform empowers you to actively engage with your professional records. You can view attendance entries recorded by the administration, ensuring accuracy and transparency. Lesson notes can be uploaded directly through your dashboard, allowing the school to keep a well-organized academic archive. By using this system, you contribute to a more efficient workflow and better communication between staff and school management.

Encouragement

We value the commitment and professionalism of our staff. This portal is part of our ongoing effort to create a supportive and organized working environment where every staff member’s contribution is recognized and properly documented.

Encouragement

At Sacred Harvesters International Christian School, we deeply appreciate the dedication, discipline, and passion our staff bring to their roles. This portal is a reflection of our commitment to supporting you with modern tools that recognize your efforts. Every attendance record and lesson submission helps document your contribution to the growth and success of our students and the institution as a whole.

Call To Action

Please sign in to access your dashboard or sign up if you are a newly appointed staff member. If you experience any issues accessing your account, kindly contact the school administration for assistance.

Call To Action

We encourage all staff members to make full use of this portal to stay informed and organized. Kindly sign in to access your dashboard and manage your records. Newly appointed staff members should complete the sign-up process to get started. If you encounter any challenges while using the system, please reach out to the school administration for prompt assistance and support.
